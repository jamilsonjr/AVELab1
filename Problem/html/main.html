Power consumption

In this section it is of interest to compare the power consumption between the different light duty urban vehicles presented in the figures (1), (2), (3) and (4) of the Lab script. To compare these, it is used two diferent driving cycles:

Basic urbarn driving cycle

clear
clc
[basic_time, basic_velocity] = BasicDrivingCycle();
figure();
plot(basic_time, basic_velocity);
grid on;
title('Basic urban driving cycle');
xlabel('time [s]');
ylabel('Velocity [v]');

Mix driving cycle

Note that the Mix driving cycle is composed of urban and extra-urban driving cycles, being the first the concatenation of basic urban driving cycles.

[mix_time, mix_velocity] = MixDrivingCycle(basic_time, basic_velocity);
figure()
plot(mix_time, mix_velocity);
grid on;
title('Basic urban driving cycle');
xlabel('time $[s]$','Interpreter', 'latex');
ylabel('Velocity [$v$]','Interpreter', 'latex');
